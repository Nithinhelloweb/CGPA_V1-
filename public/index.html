    SGPA CALCULATOR

    About:

  • SGPA is the average grade point earned in a single semester.
  • It is calculated based on the grades and credits of all subjects in that semester.
  • Formula:
    • SGPA = ∑(Credit × Grade Point) ∑Credits

    CGPA CALCULATOR

    About:

  • CGPA is the overall average of SGPAs over all completed semesters.
  • It gives your academic performance across the entire course.
  • Formula:
    • CGPA= ∑(SGPA of each semester x Credits of that semester) ∑Total Credits
